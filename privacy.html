Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Grid Munch handles information when you use the game. If you have questions, contact us at 508923216@qq.com.

Information We Store Locally

Grid Munch stores gameplay progress, level completion, star ratings, scores, and audio settings on your device. This information is used only to keep your game progress and preferences available between sessions.

Advertising And SDKs

Grid Munch may use advertising and security SDKs to show ads, request ad configuration, protect the app, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, and improve reliability. These SDKs may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate device information, app interaction data, diagnostics, and network-related information as permitted by your device settings and applicable law.

App Tracking Transparency

Where required, the app may ask for your permission before accessing the advertising identifier for tracking purposes. You can allow or deny this permission in the iOS system prompt, and you can change your choice later in iOS Settings.

Children

Grid Munch is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review the request.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Advertising and security partners may process information to provide their services, including ad delivery, fraud prevention, measurement, and app security.

Data Retention

Local gameplay progress remains on your device until you delete the app or clear the app data. Information processed by third-party SDKs is handled according to their own retention practices and applicable law.

Your Choices

You can disable personalized tracking through iOS privacy settings. You can also delete local gameplay data by removing the app from your device.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
